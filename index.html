Ordering Information

1. Choose your flavour from our menu list and let us know by filling our Order form below and we will get in touch with you within 72 hrs.(Via EMAIL) In case of pastry goods please mention them in the message tab.
2. For customized cakes, we would require atleast 7 days prior notification so that we can work out on the theme and design.
- Regular cakes (Chocolate or butter cream) please give us a space of 3 days atleast.
- Outstation orders please give us 3 days notice so that we can make sure that the cake reaches promptly.

Shipping Details

* Our Tags, Banners,Jewellery Products can be shipped all over India.
